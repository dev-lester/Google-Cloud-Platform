About me

I'm Lester Sales. I live in Quezon City, PH with my father, sister and my crappy computer.
I call myself as a software developer and I mostly code in JavaScript because I don't know I just
love it even though I haven't started a job in software development since I graduated in college.

    Why do you blog?

    Mostly for selfish reasons. I needed a way to keep track of software development over time –
    whatever I am thinking about or working on. I research things I find interesting, then document my
    research with a public blog post, which I can easily find and refer to later. Hopefully other people
    will find these posts helpful, relevant, or interesting. I firmly believe that blogs are a two way
    conversation, so I welcome email and comments – as long as they're on topic, more or less.
